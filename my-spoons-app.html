Nest Mode
when spoons are very low
You don't need to understand it right now.
Just follow the next step. One thing at a time.
1
Earbuds in. Right now, before anything else. Reduce what your system has to process.
2
Go to your softest spot. Blanket on. Pillow against your front. Hood up if you have one.
3
Drink water. Eat something small if you haven't. Your body was working hard.
4
One familiar song. Not a playlist. Something your brain already knows — just rest inside it.
5
Stim freely. Feet, fingers, face. Your body is regulating. Don't interrupt it.
6
Phone face down. Messages can wait. You are not available right now. That is allowed.
7
Stay until the static quiets. Not until a timer goes off — your body will tell you when it's ready.
